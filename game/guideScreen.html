How it started...

After travelling for several months from one solar system to the next one with his rocketship, Anthony fell in a dangerous spacestorm that took him completely off course and dropped him at a foreign planet.

Afraid of crashing with his rocketship, he ejected off it as soon as he lost control of it in the thick atmosphere of the unknown planet.

Now he has to navigate through this Martian like landscape, explore, find and repair his rocketship so that he may resume his journey.

The planet's oxygen is really sparse so if it wasn't for Anthony's ingenuity building a small base for oxygen replenishment he would die of asphyxiation really soon.

How to...

navigate Anthony through the REDET-521 planet?

Use the buttons W, A, S, D to move him respectively up, left, right and down. Zoom in/out with the mousewheel.

Explore, beware of your oxygen supply and keep your distance from any hostile inhabitants!
